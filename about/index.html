About Me

Hello! I am a PhD Research Scholar in Department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Bombay working with Dr. Manas Thakur. I work in the area of compiler optimizations and program analysis. Currently I am working to design a speculative static+dynamic program analysis technique for Java’s JIT compiler.

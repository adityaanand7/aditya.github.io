Hello! I am a 2nd year (MS+PhD) Research Scholar in School of Computing & Electrical Engineering in Computer Science programme at IIT Mandi. My Research interests lie primarily in the area of compilers and programming languages. Currently, I am working with Dr. Manas Thakur at CompL, to improve the efficiency of program analysis using "Speculative Optimizations" for JIT Compilers.
